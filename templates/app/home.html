Our Mission

KeYCo’s mission is to bridge educational gaps in Rwanda by providing access to high-quality learning resources, training educators, and enabling students to learn, collaborate, and thrive in a digital world.

Our Vision

We envision a Rwanda where every student, regardless of background or resources, has equal access to education and technology, allowing them to reach their full potential and contribute positively to society.
